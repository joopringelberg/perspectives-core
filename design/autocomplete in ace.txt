AUTOCOMPLETE IN ACE

In 
	bower_components/purescript-ace/example/src/Main.purs 
is een voorbeeld van completion te vinden:

  Session.setMode "ace/mode/text" session
  languageTools <- LanguageTools.languageTools
  Editor.setEnableBasicAutocompletion true editor
  completer <-
    Completer.mkCompleter
    (\_ _ _ inp cb -> do
        cb $ pure [ { value: inp <> "!!"
                    , score: 100.0
                    , caption: pure "???"
                    , meta: "!!"
                    }
                  , { value: "abcde"
                    , score: 200.0
                    , caption: Nothing
                    , meta: "abcde"
                    } ] )
  LanguageTools.addCompleter completer languageTools
