SYNTAX FOR QUERIES

ComputedRole = RolePath | RolePath RoleFunctionExpression
RolePath = Role [binding* | binding+ 'context' rolePath]
Role = 'ExternalRole' | <roleName>
RoleFunctionExpression = 
	'filter' PropertyExpression |
	'concat' RolePath+ |
	'closure'

Examples of RolePath:

	rol1
	rol1 binding
	rol1 binding context rol2
	rol1 binding context ExternalRole
	rol1 'filter' <propExpr> 
	rol1 binding context rol2 'filter' <propExpr> 
	
PropertyExpression = 'property' <propertyName>

Examples of a Path:

	ExternalRole property $functional
	$teamlid binding context ExternalRole property bedrijfsnaam
	$verdachte

ComputedRole = Path | LetExpression
LetExpression = 'let' VarBinding+
